About Rosstown Family Clinic

Established in 1974 by the late Dr Emmanuel Patrick, and one of the oldest and longest running family practices in Melbourne, the Rosstown Family Medical Centre (formerly Rosstown Medical Clinic) is situated at number 73 Rosstown Rd, Carnegie.

Our friendly team of doctors and helpful staff provide a broad range of general practice and clinical services and has been a loyal provider of the highest standard of medical service to the Carnegie community and surrounding areas for over 40 years. All our services are completely bulk-billed and we welcome both new and long-standing clients alike.
